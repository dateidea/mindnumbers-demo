A letter from our founder

A note from Peter

MindNumbers grew out of my own family's experience with bipolar disorder, and a career spent learning that real progress in medicine almost always starts with better measurement.

We built this app to help individuals and families recognise change sooner, act earlier, and stay safer, so people can focus on living, not logging.

The faces below belong to a small but mighty team who bring clinical depth, design care, and a lot of heart to everything we do.

We're glad you're here.

Warmly,
Peter Westlake
Peter Westlake
Founder & CEO, MindNumbers

Peter Westlake
Founder & CEO
Peter spent his career in diagnostics helping clinicians measure what was previously invisible, most recently at ProciseDx, where he led the company through two FDA approvals. When a family member was diagnosed with bipolar disorder, he set out to bring that same kind of measurement to mental health. He serves as Treasurer of the International Bipolar Foundation.
Ciarán Friel
Monitoring & App Design
Ciarán is a digital health researcher with 20+ publications on remote monitoring, and a Visiting Research Scientist at Northwell Health's Feinstein Institutes. He helps make sure MindNumbers feels light to use and rigorous underneath.
Serena Chaudhry
Scientific & Clinical Advisor
Serena is a clinical social worker at the Tulane Psychiatry Clinic and co-founder of EPIC-NOLA, an early-intervention program for serious mental illness. She keeps our work grounded in what people and families actually need when things get hard.
Maria Fernanda Currea
